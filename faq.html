Frequently asked questions

1. What exactly does The Dream Revelator do?

The Dream Revelator offers individual dream interpretations based on traditional symbolic reading. You describe your dream, how you felt when you woke up, and optional context about your life. A unique written interpretation is then created and displayed on the site.

2. Is this fortune telling?

No. The service does not predict the future and does not promise specific events. The focus is on symbolism, inner meaning and spiritual direction, not on fortune telling or guaranteed outcomes.

3. How much does one interpretation cost?

Each dream interpretation costs $3.99 (USD). It is a one-time payment for a single reading. There are no subscriptions or recurring charges.

4. Will my dream be stored permanently?

No. To allow you to view and share your interpretation, it is stored temporarily for 3 days. After that, the interpretation and related dream data are automatically deleted.

5. Do I need an account or email?

No account and no email are required to receive a reading. You complete the payment, view your interpretation on the site, and optionally share the link with someone you trust.

6. Can I share my dream interpretation?

Yes. After your reading appears, you will see an option to copy or share the link. Anyone with the link can view that interpretation during the 3-day availability period.

7. Is this psychological or religious counseling?

No. The interpretations are symbolic and spiritual in tone. They are not a substitute for medical, psychological, legal or financial advice. If you are facing serious difficulties, you should also seek appropriate professional support.
