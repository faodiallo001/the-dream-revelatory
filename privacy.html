Privacy policy

Last updated: 2025


The Dream Revelator is committed to respecting your privacy and treating your information with care and discretion. This policy explains how we handle the data you provide when using the dream interpretation service.

1. Information we collect

When you submit a dream for interpretation, we may collect:

  • the dream description you provide;
  • your emotional state upon waking;
  • optional context about your personal life;
  • basic technical information such as device type or browser.

We do not require account creation or email submission to receive a dream interpretation.

2. How your information is used

Your dream information is used solely to:

  • create a personalized symbolic interpretation;
  • display that interpretation on the result page;
  • allow you to share the interpretation link if you choose.

We do not use your dream content for targeted advertising or commercial profiling.

3. Temporary storage

To let you view and share your interpretation, your dream and its reading are stored for a limited period of 3 days. After this time, the data is automatically deleted from our systems.

We do not keep a long-term archive of individual dreams or build personal history profiles.

4. Sharing

You may decide to share your interpretation link with others. Anyone with the link can view the interpretation while it is available. We do not publish your dreams publicly on our own initiative.

5. Payments

Payments are processed securely by independent payment providers (such as Stripe). We do not store your full payment details.

6. Data security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect the temporary information you provide. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s privacy

The service is intended for users aged 16 and above. If you are under 16, you should use the service only with the consent of a parent or legal guardian.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date indicates the most recent version.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy policy, you may contact: support@thedreamrevelator.com
